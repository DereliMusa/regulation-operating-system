# Pricing (business reference)

Pricing is a **business reference**, not an MVP feature. The Pricing page ships in Phase 1.
These figures come from the marketing mockups (`eski-veriler/certa-web-sitesi/
certra_pricing_optimized_layout/`) and remain subject to change.

## Model

Tiered SaaS subscription + AI usage credits + services.

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Target | Highlights |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | EUR 0 | - | Spin-off / trial | Documents, read, limited storage, signing |
| **Starter** | EUR 499 | EUR 5,490 | SME / startup | GSPR, traceability, CER, PMS, PMCF, CAPA, audit; AI copilot (low quota); 3 technical files |
| **Professional** (most popular) | EUR 1,299 | EUR 14,299 | Larger manufacturer | Multi-site, API, SSO, custom workflows, validation; 10 technical files; advanced AI |
| **Enterprise** | EUR 3,599+ | Custom | Global org | Full customization, 24/7 support, dedicated account manager, 25+ files, unlimited AI, on-prem option |

## Add-ons

- **AI credit packs:** ~EUR 100 per 1,000 AI transactions (pay-as-you-go on top of tier quota).
- **Services:** setup / migration / training / validation support.

## Notes

- The mockup contains mixed Turkish/English labels and is a layout test; treat copy as
  provisional.
- Feature gating (files count, AI quota, API/SSO, multi-site, support level) maps to the
  comparison table on the Pricing page and must stay consistent with what the app actually
  enforces once billing exists (Phase 2).
- Revenue targets and per-customer averages cited in the TUBITAK application are
  assumptions to validate; see [`../05-references/regulatory-references.md`](../05-references/regulatory-references.md).
