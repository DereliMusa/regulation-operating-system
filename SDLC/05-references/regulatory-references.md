# Regulatory References

Domain background for the product. This is orientation for the team, **not** legal or
regulatory advice. Always defer to the official texts and qualified regulatory experts.

## Core regulations

- **MDR** — Regulation (EU) 2017/745 on medical devices.
- **IVDR** — Regulation (EU) 2017/746 on in-vitro diagnostic medical devices.

## Key standards referenced by the product

- **ISO 13485** — Quality management systems for medical devices.
- **ISO 14971:2019** — Application of risk management to medical devices.
- **IEC 62366-1** — Usability engineering.
- **IEC 62304** — Medical device software lifecycle processes.
- **MEDDEV 2.7/1 Rev. 4** — Clinical evaluation guidance.

## Concepts modeled in Certra

- **GSPR** — General Safety and Performance Requirements (MDR Annex I). Each requirement is
  mapped to evidence with a conformity status.
- **Technical file** — the complete documentation set for a device.
- **Device class** — MDR: I / IIa / IIb / III; IVDR: A / B / C / D. Higher class = higher
  scrutiny.
- **Notified Body (NB)** — the conformity-assessment body (e.g. TUV SUD, BSI, DEKRA).
- **CER** — Clinical Evaluation Report.
- **PMS / PMCF / PSUR** — Post-Market Surveillance / Post-Market Clinical Follow-up /
  Periodic Safety Update Report.
- **UDI / EUDAMED** — Unique Device Identification / the EU device database (Phase 2
  integration).
- **CAPA** — Corrective and Preventive Action (Phase 2 module).
