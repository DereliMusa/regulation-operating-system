Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data secured in the cloud? expand_more

Certra utilizes AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. We are ISO 27001 certified and perform quarterly third-party penetration tests to ensure the integrity of your clinical data. Each Enterprise client receives a physically isolated database instance.

Can we deploy Certra on our own servers? expand_more

Yes, our Enterprise plan supports on-premise deployment via Docker/Kubernetes or within your private VPC (AWS, Azure, or GCP). This allows for maximum control over data sovereignty while maintaining full access to Certra's automated updates and AI modules.

What happens if we run out of AI Tokens? expand_more

Growth plan subscribers can purchase additional token packs at any time from the Dashboard. If you find your usage consistently exceeds the limit, our team can help you transition to an Enterprise plan with custom token allocations or unlimited access.
